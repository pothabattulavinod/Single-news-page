Karthikeya 2 Wins National Award for Best Telugu Film

The 70th National Film Awards recognized Karthikeya 2 as the Best Regional Telugu Film. The movie, released in 2022, is a sequel to the 2014 hit Karthikeya, and it captivated audiences with its gripping story and stellar performances.

Directed by Chandoo Mondeti and starring Nikhil Siddhartha, the film achieved national acclaim, highlighting the growing prominence of Telugu cinema across India.
